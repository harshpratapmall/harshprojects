TROPHY CARRIES THE DUST

"Winners never quit, and quitters never win"

Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technolgy offers wide scope for the students to excel in the domain of sports and games. The students play wide variety of sports and games such as Cricket, Kabaddi, Football, Badminton, Basketball, Table tennis, Volleyball, Chess, Athletics etc. Being one of the prominent Technical Universities in Uttar Pradesh, Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology has never compromised in providing the facilities required to enhance the students' talent. A well-equipped Gymnasium is present at University Stadium to ensure students' fitness and sound health. Equipments of Cricket , Volleyball, Basketball and Indoor courts etc. are provided to the students.
