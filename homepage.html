ATHLETICS

IIT Bhubaneswar offer extravagant facilities to enhance the student's Athletic spirit . Both Track and Field Athletics is encouraged from Students . The Institute Athletics Team actively participates in Inter IIT every year to enhance the institute pride among all IITs . The Students are regularly trained in various events like Sprint ,Hurdles ,LongJump ,HighJump ,TripleJump ,Shotput ,DiscusThrow ,HammerThrow and JavelinThrow.
The Athletes over the institute have access to participate in Athletic Sports Fests and Tournaments all across the country. Apart from Inter IIT, Various Inter Departmental and Inter Hall Tournaments are organised to raise the competitive spirit among students. The Gymkhana functions with the motto to maintain the student ,physically fit throughout the year.

CRICKET

Cricket was first played in England in the 16th century. By the end of the 18th century, it had developed to be the national sport of England.The first international cricket match was held in mid 19 th century. Cricket is the most popular sport in India. The Indian national cricket team won the 1983 Cricket World Cup, the 2007 ICC World Twenty20 and the 2011 Cricket World Cup. The Indian Premier League (IPL) is the most-watched Twenty20 cricket league in the world and is also known for its commercial success. Cricket is surely one of the most loved sports of the country and the IIT BHUBANESWAR Cricket society was formed to encourage students in the institute to love this enthusiastic sport. With all year round events and activities in the form of INTER IIT, GENERAL CHAMPIONSHIP, HOSTEL DAY GAMES and a lot more, the cricket society of IIT BHUBANESWAR keeps the sport lovers active with a combination of fun events and serious sport.

FOOTBALL

"Football is like life - it requires perseverance, self-denial, hard word,sacrifice, dedication and respect for authority"

Our campus has a football ground equipped with the necessary requirements. Every year we organise hostel day games in which a student bids for players and forms his team and then the fellow students compete for the winner's title. Along with this every year we also organise General Championship in which every Department build their team and compete with each other to see their department lift the Rolling trophy. Apart from this, from this year onwards we are taking an initiative to organise Mahanadi Premier League(MPL). This year we have vowed to practise regularly so that we can leave an impactat the Inter-IIT Sports meet this year at IIT Kanpur.Other than Inter-IIT we also participate in sports fests of colleges in and outside Bhubaneswar.
